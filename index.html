What the audit measures

Five signals AI uses to decide which practices to recommend

Your star rating is one input among many — and it's the least differentiating. These are the gaps that actually determine whether an AI names your practice or skips it.

01
Review Velocity Volume & recency

How many reviews you earn per month versus the top operators in your local market. AI systems weight recency — a stagnant profile quietly recedes from recommendation results.

02
Owner Response Rate Indexed content

The percentage of reviews — positive and negative — that receive a substantive owner response. Responses are indexed content. Silence is also a signal, and AI reads both.

03
Review Content Quality Specificity

What percentage of your reviews name a specific service, practitioner, or outcome. Generic "great place!" reviews are noise to a language model. Specific reviews are citations.

04
AI Sentiment Score Language model read

How your full review corpus reads to an AI — the overall tone, trustworthiness signals, and specificity in the language your patients use to describe their experience.

05
Competitive Benchmark Your market

Where you rank on all four dimensions against the leaders and laggards in your specific city. You cannot close a gap you cannot see — and most owners have never looked.
